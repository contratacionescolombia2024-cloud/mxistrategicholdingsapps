
# User Guide: KYC Verification Process

## What is KYC?

KYC (Know Your Customer) is a verification process required by financial regulations to confirm your identity. This one-time verification is mandatory before you can withdraw funds from your account.

## Why is KYC Required?

- **Security**: Protects your account from unauthorized access
- **Compliance**: Meets international financial regulations
- **Fraud Prevention**: Helps prevent money laundering and fraud
- **Account Protection**: Ensures only you can access your funds

## What You'll Need

### Required Information
- Full legal name (as it appears on your ID)
- Government-issued ID document
- Document number from your ID

### Accepted Documents
1. **National ID Card**
   - Front and back photos required
   - Must be valid (not expired)
   - All text must be clearly readable

2. **Passport**
   - Photo page required
   - Must be valid (not expired)
   - All text must be clearly readable

3. **Driver's License**
   - Front and back photos required
   - Must be valid (not expired)
   - All text must be clearly readable

### Photo Requirements
- **Clear and Readable**: All text must be legible
- **Good Lighting**: Avoid shadows and glare
- **Full Document**: Entire document must be visible
- **No Obstructions**: Don't cover any part of the document
- **File Size**: Under 10MB per image
- **Format**: JPEG, PNG, or WebP

## Step-by-Step Guide

### Step 1: Access KYC Verification
1. Open the app
2. Go to your Profile
3. Tap on "KYC Verification"

### Step 2: Check Your Status
You'll see one of these statuses:
- **Not Submitted**: You haven't submitted KYC yet
- **Under Review**: Your KYC is being reviewed by admin
- **Approved**: Your KYC is verified ✅
- **Rejected**: Your KYC was rejected (see reason below)

### Step 3: Fill Out Personal Information
1. **Full Legal Name**
   - Enter your name exactly as it appears on your ID
   - Include middle names if shown on ID
   - Use proper capitalization

2. **Document Type**
   - Select the type of ID you're submitting
   - Choose: National ID, Passport, or Driver's License

3. **Document Number**
   - Enter the ID number from your document
   - Double-check for accuracy
   - Use capital letters if applicable

### Step 4: Upload Documents

#### For National ID or Driver's License:

**Front of Document**
1. Tap "Tap to upload front"
2. Select a clear photo of the front of your ID
3. Wait for "✅ Upload Successful" message
4. You can tap again to change the photo if needed

**Back of Document**
1. Tap "Tap to upload back"
2. Select a clear photo of the back of your ID
3. Wait for "✅ Upload Successful" message
4. You can tap again to change the photo if needed

#### For Passport:
1. Tap "Tap to upload front"
2. Select a clear photo of your passport photo page
3. Wait for "✅ Upload Successful" message
4. Back photo is not required for passports

### Step 5: Review and Submit
1. Double-check all information is correct
2. Ensure both documents are uploaded (unless passport)
3. Tap "Submit KYC Verification"
4. Read the confirmation message
5. Tap "Submit" to confirm

### Step 6: Wait for Review
After submission:
- Your status will change to "Under Review"
- Admin will review within 24-48 hours
- You'll receive a notification when reviewed
- Check back to see your status

## What Happens Next?

### If Approved ✅
- You'll receive an approval notification
- Your status will show "Approved"
- You can proceed with withdrawals (if other requirements are met)
- Verification date will be displayed

### If Rejected ❌
- You'll receive a rejection notification
- The reason for rejection will be shown
- You can review the reason
- You can resubmit with corrected documents

## Common Rejection Reasons

1. **Blurry or Unclear Photos**
   - Solution: Take new photos in good lighting
   - Ensure all text is readable

2. **Expired Document**
   - Solution: Use a valid, non-expired ID
   - Check expiration date before submitting

3. **Incomplete Document**
   - Solution: Ensure entire document is visible
   - Don't crop or cut off any edges

4. **Name Mismatch**
   - Solution: Enter name exactly as on ID
   - Include all names shown on document

5. **Wrong Document Type**
   - Solution: Select correct document type
   - Ensure document matches selection

6. **Poor Quality**
   - Solution: Use better camera or lighting
   - Avoid shadows and glare
   - Take photo on flat surface

## Tips for Success

### Taking Good Photos
1. **Use Good Lighting**
   - Natural daylight is best
   - Avoid direct flash
   - No shadows on document

2. **Flat Surface**
   - Place document on flat, contrasting surface
   - Dark document on light surface or vice versa
   - Avoid wrinkled or damaged documents

3. **Steady Camera**
   - Hold phone steady or use a stand
   - Take multiple photos and choose best one
   - Ensure focus is sharp

4. **Full Document**
   - Include all four corners
   - Don't crop any edges
   - Capture entire document in frame

5. **Readable Text**
   - All text must be clearly legible
   - Numbers must be distinguishable
   - Photo must be in focus

## Frequently Asked Questions

### How long does review take?
Typically 24-48 hours. You'll receive a notification when reviewed.

### Can I edit after submitting?
No, but if rejected, you can resubmit with corrections.

### What if I don't have these documents?
Contact support for alternative verification methods.

### Is my data secure?
Yes, all data is encrypted and stored securely. We comply with international data protection regulations.

### Can I withdraw before KYC approval?
No, KYC approval is required for all withdrawals.

### What if my document is in another language?
Documents in most languages are accepted. If there's an issue, admin will contact you.

### Do I need to verify again?
No, KYC is a one-time verification process.

### What if I change my name?
Contact support to update your information with new documents.

## Troubleshooting

### Upload Fails
**Problem**: Document won't upload
**Solutions**:
- Check internet connection
- Ensure file is under 10MB
- Try a different photo
- Use JPEG or PNG format
- Restart the app

### Submit Button Doesn't Work
**Problem**: Can't submit KYC
**Solutions**:
- Ensure all fields are filled
- Check both documents are uploaded
- Verify you're logged in
- Try logging out and back in

### Status Not Updating
**Problem**: Status still shows old information
**Solutions**:
- Pull down to refresh
- Close and reopen the app
- Check your internet connection
- Wait a few minutes and check again

### Can't See Documents
**Problem**: Uploaded documents don't show
**Solutions**:
- Wait for upload to complete
- Check for "Upload Successful" message
- Try uploading again
- Check internet connection
